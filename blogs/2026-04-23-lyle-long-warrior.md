Lyle Long Warrior Himel, Rikki L Thu, 04/23/2026 - 21:47 Lyle Long Warrior went missing on May 29, 1994, and was last seen near the Missouri River from the railroad bridge in Pierre, SD. Lyle was 6 years old at the time he went missing and underwater and ground search efforts have been conducted with no results and Lyle remains missing. The Bureau of Indian Affairs Missing and Murdered Unit and Pierre Police Department are seeking information that may assist them with the ongoing search.
