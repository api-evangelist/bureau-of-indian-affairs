Alaska Bureau of Indian Affairs Probate & Enrollment Aguilar, Fermin E Fri, 05/01/2026 - 16:56 Location and Date Location Date Time Egan Convention Center 555 W 5th Ave Anchorage, AK 99501 Tuesday, May 19th 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM Supporting Alaska Native & American Indian Trust Estates The BIA Alaska Region invites Alaska Native families to a community event supporting the federal probate process for trust estates. Probate & Estate Services staff will help families complete the Family History Questionnaire and gather key documents, such as birth, death, marriage, adoption, and cultural adoption records, to reduce delays and support timely probate review. Enrollment, Realty and Land Titles and Records staff will provide land inventories and answer questions about trust, CDIBs or restricted land interests.
